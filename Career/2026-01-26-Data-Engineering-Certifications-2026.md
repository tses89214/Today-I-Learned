3. dbt Analytics Engineering Certification
Most pipeline failures occur during the transformation stage, often due to inefficient joins or ambiguous business logic. Data engineers frequently spend excessive time ensuring these pipelines run correctly. Holding a dbt certification shows you have "battlefield experience"—you understand the importance of tools that manage complex dependencies and save valuable engineering hours.
